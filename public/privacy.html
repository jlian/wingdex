WingDex Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Data We Process

WingDex stores birding records you create (such as outings, observations, and related metadata) to provide app functionality.

2. Photo Handling

Photos used for identification are processed by server-side AI endpoints and are not retained after processing, except as needed for active request handling.

3. Authentication Data

Authentication data is processed through configured identity methods (such as passkeys and social providers) to manage account access and security.

4. Third-Party Services

WingDex may use third-party services for species media, geocoding, and hosting infrastructure. Those services may process limited request data.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained while your account remains active, unless removal is requested or required by law.

6. Security

Reasonable measures are used to protect data, but no method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Changes and Contact

This policy may be updated over time. For privacy questions, contact the WingDex project maintainer via the GitHub repository issue tracker.
